Help Center (India)

Indian-specific FAQs and support.

How do I pay via UPI on TRIVER India?

Select UPI as payment method during booking, enter your UPI ID (e.g., 1234567890@upi), and complete payment via your UPI app. You will receive a payment confirmation SMS.

What if my ride is delayed during monsoon?

Monsoon delays are common in Indian cities. You can cancel the ride for free if the driver is more than 15 minutes late, or wait for a 10% discount on the final fare.

How to become a driver partner in India?

Visit our Drive With Us page, fill the application form with your driver's license, Aadhar, PAN, and vehicle RC details. Our team will verify your documents within 3 working days.

SOS button not working?

Call our toll-free number +91 1800 123 4567 immediately. For emergencies, dial 100 (Police) or 102 (Ambulance) directly. Ensure your app has location permissions enabled.

Can I book rides for someone else in India?

Yes, enter the passenger's name and phone number during booking. The driver will call the passenger directly. You can pay via your UPI ID for the ride.
