About TRIVER India

Redefining Indian mobility since 2023.

Our Story

Founded in Bangalore in 2023, TRIVER India was born out of a need for premium, safe, and affordable mobility for Indian commuters. We started with 10 drivers in Bangalore and now serve 10+ cities with 5000+ active drivers.

Our mission is to make daily commuting stress-free for Indians, with a focus on safety, transparency, and local relevance.

Mission

To provide India's most reliable premium ride-booking experience with 100% verified drivers and transparent INR pricing.

Vision

To be the #1 mobility platform in India, serving 50+ cities with electric vehicle options by 2028.
