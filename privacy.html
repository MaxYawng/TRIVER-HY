Privacy Policy (India)

Last Updated: May 8, 2026

TRIVER India complies with the Indian Digital Personal Data Protection Act (DPDP) 2023. This policy explains how we collect, use, and store your personal data in India.

1. Data Collection

We collect your name, phone number, UPI ID, location data, and ride history. For drivers, we collect Aadhar, PAN, driver's license, and vehicle RC details.

2. Data Storage

All user data is stored on Indian servers located in Bangalore and Mumbai, as per DPDP Act requirements. We do not transfer data outside India without your explicit consent.

3. Data Usage

Your data is used to provide ride services, process UPI payments, send ride updates via WhatsApp/SMS, and improve our platform. We do not sell your data to third parties.

4. Consent for Aadhar

Driver partners must provide explicit consent for us to verify their Aadhar details with UIDAI, as per Indian law. You can withdraw consent at any time by emailing privacy@triverindia.com.

5. Data Security

We use AES-256 encryption for all data storage, and TLS 1.3 for all data transmission. Only authorized TRIVER India employees have access to your personal data.
